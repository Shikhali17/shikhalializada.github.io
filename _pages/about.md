Gulnara Zaynutdinova, Ph.D., is an Associate Professor of Finance at the [John Chambers College of Business and Economics](https://business.wvu.edu/) at [West Virginia University](https://www.wvu.edu/).

Her research is in empirical asset pricing, with emphasis on institutional investors, market efficiency, corporate governance, responsible investing, and energy finance.

She teaches Financial Management, Financial Institutions, Intermediate Finance, Investments, and doctoral-level Investments.
